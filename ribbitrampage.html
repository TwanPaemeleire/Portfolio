Ribbit Rampage

Ribbit Rampage is a chaotic co-op boss fighting game for two players with an emphasis on working together. It was made over the course of a semester as part of my study programme at Howest - Digital Arts and Entertainment in a group of three programmers and two artists.

Personal Contribution

Worked on the boss fight attack logic
Implemented a ton of models, sprites, SFX and RFX
Implemented the pickups and the interactions they have with both the players
Made health functionality for the player, and boss (which works for multiple different phases)
Implemented co-op functionality like handling multiple inputs or a join screen
Lots of general polishing like screenshake, making the player/boss flash red when taking damage, etc.

Technologies Used

Unity 6000 with C#
Visual Studio as IDE
HacknPlan for distributing tasks and logging work times
Perforce for version control and collaboration
