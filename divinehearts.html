Divine Hearts

Divine Hearts is a 2D story-driven boss-rush game, made in Unity for the Brackeys Jam 2025-2. We worked on this game with 2 programmers, 1 artist, 1 sound designer and 1 music composer over the course of a single week.

At this time, the game jam is in its rating phase, as such, our placement in it is currently unknown.
